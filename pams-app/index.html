Templates & Guidance

Start by downloading one of the templates. Populate the required columns and then upload your CSV file.

  • Use the external template for customer-facing workstreams and the internal template for presales enablement or back-office tasks.
  • Activity Category will auto-populate based on the template filename—feel free to leave that column blank.
  • Each CSV includes comment rows with key reminders; they are ignored during import.
  • Multi-select values (Products, Channels, Use Cases) should be pipe separated, e.g. WhatsApp | Web. Reference lists are available in the Field Value download.
