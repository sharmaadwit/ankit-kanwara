# Testing: Reports visibility (`reports`)

## What it controls

**Reports** navigation and reports view access (used with `csvExport` for export actions).

## Enable

Matrix row **Reports** ON.

## Verify (on)

- Sidebar **Reports** visible; reports view loads (card or classic UI).

## Verify (off)

- **Reports** nav hidden; navigation to reports blocked.

## Note

Analytics-only users may still be routed to reports by auth rules — confirm behaviour for your roles after toggling.
